A misfit teen animation world about becoming visible before you are ready.

When a group of outcasts wake with impossible wings, they turn their small town into a test site for fame, fear and the messy business of growing up in public.

World

The Wing-Ed Freaks is built around characters who are funny, sharp and deeply exposed. The wings are not a superpower fantasy. They are a social event: a visible difference that turns school corridors, group chats and local news into pressure chambers.

Audience route

The project is designed for young adult audiences who move naturally between episodes, character fragments, playable moments and audio-led story clues. Each format expands the same emotional engine rather than repeating the same plot.

Partner opportunity

Development materials include a series bible, pilot outline, character dossiers, game-loop notes and a compact proof-of-world package for funders and commissioning partners.
