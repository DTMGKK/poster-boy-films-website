About the world

The Wing-Ed Freaks is an original story world developed by Poster Boy Films, built from the ground up for young adult audiences who expect their entertainment to follow them across platforms. The story centres on a group of misfits navigating a world that doesn't quite fit them — and building something new in the gaps.

The world has a specific visual language, a founding mythology, and a set of characters designed for long-form development. It is not a single-format project. It is a story ecosystem.

The story

In a world where the rules are set by people who stopped paying attention, a loose collective of misfits, makers and outliers begins to build something different. The Wing-Ed Freaks aren't trying to change the world. They're trying to live honestly inside it.

The animation series follows the core ensemble across three seasons. The game extends the world in a parallel timeline. The audio drama digs into individual characters' backstories. Each format is designed to stand alone, and to reward audiences who move between all three.

Format strategy

01
Animation Series
3-season arc, 10×22min per season. The primary format and anchor of the world.
02
Game
A narrative exploration game developed with Adventureverse Studios. Parallel story timeline, player agency built in.
03
Audio Drama
Character-focus episodes. Designed for podcast distribution, standalone listening, and world-depth.
04

Graphic novel and companion world material, aligned with series release windows.
